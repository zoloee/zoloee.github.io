How She Got There

An interview with Mule Design´s Erika Hall about philosophy, phone calls and her professional journey.

In the fall of 2013 I interviewed Erttta Hall, co-founder of Mule Design Studio, onest of the most influential design practices in North America. It was the first of fourteen interviews that I would conduct for my book How They Got There: Interviews With Digital Designers About Their Careers, and so in many ways it was the most important, too.
